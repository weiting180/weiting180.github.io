Default of Credit Card Customers

This project applied most major supervised machine learning models to classify those default customers based on their credit balance, age, marriage status, etc. The data is slight imbalanced, targets to non-targets is approximate 1:4, thus various scoring criteria are adopted to model evaluation for each models, and a admissible outcome is presented in the end.
